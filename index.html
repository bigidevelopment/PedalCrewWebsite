Built by a cyclist, for cyclists

Hi! I'm a Computer Science student and endurance sports enthusiast. Like you, I love gym sessions, road cycling, and running. But organizing group rides? That was always a mess.

After a year of solo development, countless late nights, and way too much coffee, PedalCrew was born. This isn't just another app—it's my solution to the chaos we all know too well.
