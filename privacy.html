Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025‑09‑23

PedalCrew is currently in a limited demo/testing phase. This website is a simple static page that does not use cookies or track you. We do not collect personal data on this website.

Mobile app

The PedalCrew Android app connects to third‑party services to provide core features. These may include Firebase services such as Authentication, Firestore/Database, Cloud Functions, Crashlytics, and optionally Analytics (only if you consent in settings). Your usage of the app is subject to the privacy policies of those providers.

What we collect in the app

  • Account basics: your Google account UID, email, display name, and optional photo URL.
  • Profile fields you provide: preferred pace, home city, analytics consent, notification preferences.
  • Ride data: rides you create or join, ride status updates, and activity feed entries.
  • Points and ranks: totals and transactions for create/join/complete actions.
  • Crash reports: if enabled, anonymized crash and performance data to improve stability.
  • Analytics: if you opt in, anonymous usage events such as login, ride create/join/leave, filter apply.

Permissions

The demo build may request network access and Google Sign‑In. We do not request location, contacts, or storage access in this demo scope.

How we use your data

  • To authenticate you and keep your account in sync across sessions.
  • To show, create, and manage rides and participation.
  • To compute points and rank levels.
  • To improve app quality via crash reports and, if enabled, analytics.

Data retention

Profile and ride data remains in the database until deleted. You can request account deletion at any time (Settings → Delete account in‑app, or email us). Crash and analytics data follow the retention policies of the underlying providers.

Your choices

  • Use the in‑app toggle to opt in/out of analytics at any time.
  • Request account deletion and associated data removal.
  • Contact us with any privacy questions.

Children’s privacy

This demo is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us to remove it.

Changes

We may update this policy as the demo evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above.

Contact

If you have questions or requests, contact us at developmentbigi@gmail.com.
